## Raw Data

The raw Chandrayaan-3 LIBS Level-1 calibrated data used in this study is
publicly available through ISRO's Planetary Data Archive (PRADAN):

- **Source:** Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
- **Dataset:** Chandrayaan-3 ChaSTE/LIBS Calibrated Data, Version 2 (`lib-v2`)
- **Level:** L1 (Calibrated)
- **Data Format:** PDS4 (XML label + CSV table pairs)
- **Access URL:** https://pradan.issdc.gov.in/
- **Access Conditions:** Free registration required. Data is publicly available
  to the global scientific community.

Specific observation folders used in this study correspond to mission dates
`20230823` through `20230902`, observation codes `ch3_lib_001` to `ch3_lib_013`
(full inventory in [`Pipeline/study_summary.json`](Pipeline/study_summary.json)).

## Processed Data

The fully processed, background-subtracted, and MD5-verified spectral records
generated by running the LunarAtlas pipeline on the raw ISRO data are archived at:

- **Repository:** Zenodo / Mendeley Data
- **DOI:** https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.[YOUR-PROCESSED-DATA-DOI]
- **Size:** ~[X] GB (N = [X] observations × [X] measurements × 2,094 channels)
- **Format:** Long-form CSV (one row per wavelength-intensity pair)

The processed dataset includes:
- `*_cleaned.csv` — background-subtracted, clamped intensity records
- `*_metadata.json` — PDS4-extracted observation metadata
- `*_instrument_params.csv` — per-measurement laser parameters
- `*_nist_verification_log.csv` — NIST peak cross-reference tables
- `checksums.md5` — MD5 digital signature manifest for all files

## NIST Reference Data

Atomic emission line reference wavelengths were obtained from the NIST Atomic
Spectra Database (ASD):

- **Source:** National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
- **URL:** https://physics.nist.gov/asd
- **Access:** Freely available, no registration required.
- **Version:** Accessed August 2024.
